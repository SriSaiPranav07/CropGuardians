How the AI Works

From a single photograph to a full clinical diagnosis in under three seconds — powered by MobileNetV2 transfer learning.

📸

📋

Tomato Late Blight
Phytophthora infestans
🍅 Tomato Fungal / Oomycete ⚠ High Severity
Disease Class
Late Blight
Plant Type
Solanum lycopersicum
Pathogen Type
Oomycete
Spread Risk
Very High
Observed Symptoms
  • Dark brown to black lesions with water-soaked borders on leaves
  • White fuzzy sporulation visible on leaf undersides in humid conditions
  • Rapid yellowing and necrosis spreading from leaf margins
  • Distinctive foul odor from affected tissue
  1. 01
    Immediate Isolation
    Remove and bag all visibly infected leaves and stems immediately. Do not compost — dispose in sealed waste bags to prevent spore spread.
  2. 02
    Fungicide Application
    Apply copper-based fungicide (e.g., Bordeaux mixture) or chlorothalonil. Spray entire plant including undersides every 7–10 days.
  3. 03
    Improve Air Circulation
    Prune dense foliage to allow airflow. Stake plants upright. Avoid overhead irrigation — water at soil level in morning hours only.
  4. 04
    Soil Treatment
    Apply organic mulch to prevent soil splash back. Rotate crops next season — avoid Solanaceae family in the same bed for 2+ years.
  5. 05
    Monitoring Protocol
    Inspect plants every 48 hours. If disease spreads beyond 30% of canopy, consider full plant removal to protect neighboring crops.

Technology Stack

Powered By

A production-grade deep learning pipeline combining computer vision, transfer learning, and scalable web inference.

🐍
Python 3.10+
Core programming language for data preprocessing, model training, and Flask backend orchestration.
🔷
TensorFlow / Keras
Deep learning framework for building, training, and serving the MobileNetV2 CNN classification model.
📱
MobileNetV2 CNN
Lightweight pre-trained architecture with depthwise separable convolutions. Trained on ImageNet, fine-tuned on 87K plant images.
👁️
OpenCV
Real-time image preprocessing pipeline: resizing to 224×224, normalization, augmentation, and format conversion.
🌐
Flask
Lightweight Python web framework serving the RESTful prediction API and rendering the frontend interface.
🔄
Transfer Learning
Last 10 layers of MobileNetV2 unfrozen for fine-tuning. Adam optimizer at 1e-4 LR with EarlyStopping callbacks.
